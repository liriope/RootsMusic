The Music

from the rapid-fire banjo picking and quirky yodeling of Raising Arizona (1987), to the deep, stirring folk songs of Inside Llewyn Davis (2013), over the course of more than three decades Joel and Ethan Coen have produced some of the most memorable and influential American roots music soundtracks in the history of film. The Coen Brothers and American Roots Music is devoted to examining and providing a full critical accounting of this valuable and rich legacy.
